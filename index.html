Our Story

Warrensburg's Favorite Donut Shop

Since opening, Yummy's Donut Palace has become the go-to morning destination for locals, students, and travelers passing through. We make everything fresh, every single day.

🌅
Baked Fresh Daily
Our donuts are made fresh every morning starting at 5 AM. Get there early — the best ones sell out fast!
😊
Famously Friendly Staff
Our team is known throughout Warrensburg for warm, welcoming service. We love families, students, and everyone in between.
🚗
Drive-Through & Dine-In
Grab your order on the go via our drive-through, or sit down in our clean, cozy space with free Wi-Fi.
📍
Now in Sedalia Too!
Loved so much we expanded — a second Yummy's location is now open in Sedalia, MO.
